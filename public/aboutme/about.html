About

TL;DR

I love programming, and I especially enjoy working with the full stack. I want to become an awesome senior-level developer. My ideal job is working with a smart and supportive team capable of turning challenge into opportunity and dedicated to improving people’s lives through software.

I am experienced in the full stack, and specifically React and Redux. I recently finished Fullstack Academy‘s remote immersive software engineering program.

I’m also a successful entrepreneur. I started and grew an e-commerce site and local retail store that I recently sold in order to return to my first true love: fullstack development. My background is in physics. I taught physics in the U.S. Navy and have a M.S. in Physics. I have an unrelenting thirst for software engineering, problem-solving, data analysis, and business – especially any business that gives back to its community and employees.

Physics

Inside the clean room with my superconducting cavity during the high-pressure rinse.

After graduating with a B.S. in Engineering Physics, I taught physics, chemistry, heat transfer, and radiological fundamentals in the U.S. Navy to 430 students during 1,500 of classroom instruction.

It was an intense time in which I mastered how to communicate and teach well, as demonstrated by my students continually performing above average.

I then studied accelerator physics and graduated with a M.S. in Accelerator Physics after I designed, fabricated, and tested a novel superconducting cavity proposed for the International Linear Collider.

Entrepreneur

Local press conference, discussing how businesses can be profitable and still provide a living wage.

I went on to start a successful e-commerce and brick and mortar business. During my twelve years of ownership, my business thrived, grew laterally and vertically, and I always tried to have a positive impact on those around me and across the country.
